Sustainable Fishing.

Conserve Coastal and Marine Areas.

Seafood that has been raised or harvested in a way that doesn't damage the environment or other wildlife is considered environmentally sustainable seafood, as it contributes to the resilience and health of ocean ecosystems. The term "wild-caught seafood" refers to seafood whose populations are responsibly managed, not overfished, and whose fishing gear has little effect on surrounding habitat and wildlife.
